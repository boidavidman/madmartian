Description

Mad Martian is a 3D third and first person shooter game taking place on mars. The game follows Ian, a stranded robot created by humans, as he does his best to survive until his creators come and rescue him.

Characters

Ian

Ian is the protagonist of Mad Martian. He is the seventh generation of the IAN (Intelligent Autonomous Neurodroid), MK7 for short.

Appearance

Ian's body is comprised of dark green metal components which are connected to one another via black flexible rubbery material that bends to protect the joints and electronic components underneath. Ian has a singular optical lens, which is built into the cyan light in his face.
